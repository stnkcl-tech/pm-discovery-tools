# Competitive Landscape

| Solution | Strengths | Gaps | Why Users Stay |
|----------|-----------|------|----------------|
| **Slack / Teams** | Ubiquitous; real-time chat | Async noise; culture becomes performative | It's where work happens |
| **Zoom Happy Hours** | Easy to schedule; face time | Zoom fatigue; excludes introverts | Cheap and familiar |
| **Donut / Random Coffee** | Automates 1:1 intros | Shallow interactions; no continuity | Low effort to maintain |
| **In-person Offsites** | High-bandwidth bonding | Expensive; infrequent; exclusionary | Nothing replicates in-person depth |
